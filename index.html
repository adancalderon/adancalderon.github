Zookeeper

Zookeeping

Simulation of running a ZOO. Specifically, assigning zoo animals to specific outdoor enclosures such that they have enough space and that they can not move into other enclosures of the zoo. random grid of 25 x 25 with probability of an obstacle(#) of 0.50. This grid represents zoo area, the regions represents the various enclosures for the animals.
